With "Career Site" you can host the jobs posted on your Peerlist company profile on your own subdomain. eg. **careers.\[company].com**

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Enter your subdomain name and click on Add domain
    <Frame>
        <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1739115880983/5f76360c-dc37-48a8-965d-d2fc277e0580.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    To verify domain ownership, please add the TXT record to your domain. Once added, click on **Verify Ownership.**
    
    <Note>
      It may take anywhere between 30-60 minutes for domain propagation.
    </Note>
    
    <Frame>
        <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1739116327997/c9ea1223-f331-40f2-89b2-ad3440b37a3a.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Add A or CNAME record to your DNS settings. Once added, click on Check Configuration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

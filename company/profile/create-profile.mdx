## Prerequisites:

1. Create a personal profile on Peerlist. In case you don't have an account, you can <a href={PEERLIST_EXTERNAL_LINKS.signup} target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>sign up here</a>

2. Get your work experience verified. In case you aren't, learn how to <a href={APP_PATHS.verification} target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>get your experience verified.</a>

## Creating a company profile:

1. Once your experience is verified, click on the **"Create Company Profile"** button on the left sidebar.

   <Frame>
    <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1737526887247/1b9688b7-e392-42c6-9247-4eac397b4209.png" />
   </Frame>

2. In the next modal, add the details of your company. If you are a bootcamp, select the checkbox at the bottom and click **"Continue"**.

   <Frame>
    <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1737527159698/819ccbb5-903e-4f1b-b7eb-4ba2c1851e70.png" />
   </Frame>

3. After a confirmation, your company profile will be created!

   <Frame>
    <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1737527341063/7fcfc8e6-dd85-47f7-ac33-33dc8a625ead.png" />
   </Frame>

4. Once the company profile is created, you can add more details to it by clicking on **"Edit profile".**

   <Frame>
    <img src="https://cdn.hashnode.com/res/hashnode/image/upload/v1737527805561/58ee94e0-1b4b-4a72-be13-937c66d3f993.png" />
   </Frame>

---
title: 'How to add courses on Bootcamp profile?'
description: 'Learn how to add courses on Bootcamp profile.'
sidebarTitle: 'Courses'
---


<Steps>
  <Step>
   Scroll down on your Bootcamp profile, till you find the “Courses” section. Click **+Add**.
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/courses/section.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
   You will be navigated to the Manage Courses page. Click **+ New Course**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/courses/add-new-course.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
   Add details for your course and **Publish**. Aim for 100% completion.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/courses/add-course-form.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
  A new course will be added to the courses section on your Bootcamp profile.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/courses/course-card.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

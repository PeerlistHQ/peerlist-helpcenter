The Peerlist Invite Friends and Earn Rewards program allows users to earn store credit by inviting new members to join Peerlist. Rewards are issued in U.S. dollars and can be redeemed as coupon credits on the <a href={PEERLIST_STORE} target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>Peerlist Store</a>.

## How It Works

* You will receive **$1** for every valid user who joins Peerlist using your unique invite link and completes their profile (min 40%).

* You will receive an **additional $1** when the invited user **completes their** <a href={APP_PATHS.verification}>**profile verification**</a>.

* Earnings are recorded in the "Rewards History" section and can be redeemed as store coupons.

## Redemption of Rewards

* Credits can **only be redeemed as coupons** on <a href={PEERLIST_STORE} target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>store.peerlist.io</a>.
* Coupon redemption must be in **multiples of \$10** (e.g., \$10, \$20, \$30, etc.).
* The **minimum redeemable amount is $10**.
* Coupons are **non-refundable, non-transferable**, and **cannot be exchanged for cash**.

## Eligibility

* The Program is available to all registered Peerlist users with an active account.

* Invited users must be **real individuals**, not spam or duplicate accounts.

* Both the inviter and invitee must **comply with Peerlist's** <a href={APP_PATHS.termConditions}>**Terms of Service**</a>.

## Program Changes

* Peerlist **reserves the right to modify, pause, or terminate the Invite Rewards Program at any time without prior notice**.

* This includes changes to reward values, redemption methods, eligibility criteria, and program rules.

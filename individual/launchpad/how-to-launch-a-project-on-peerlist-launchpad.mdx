## Prerequisites

You must have a <a href={APP_PATHS.verification}>**Verified Peerlist Profile**</a> to be able to launch a project on the Peerlist Launchpad.

## **Add a project to your profile**

* Navigate to the Work tab on your profile. (peerlist.io/\[username]/work)

* Click on "New project"

* Complete all required project information

* Ensure your project reaches 100% completion status

* Save your project

<Info>
  Only projects that are 100% complete are eligible for launching on Launchpad. Make sure all required fields are filled out and your project description is comprehensive.
</Info>

***

## Launching your project

Monday is a launch day. The launchpad is open through Mondays, every week.

### **Access Launchpad on Launch Day**

* Visit <a href={PEERLIST_EXTERNAL_LINKS.launchpad} target='_blank' rel='noopener noreferrer'>peerlist.io/launchpad</a> on Monday

* Look for the "Launch" button prominently displayed in the top header

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/launchpad/launchpad-screen.png" />
  </Frame>

### **Launching your project**

* Click on the "Launch →" button

* Select your project from the dropdown menu of eligible projects

* Click "Launch Project"

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/launchpad/project-select-modal.png" />
  </Frame>

### **Celebrate your launch**

* Your project will immediately go live on Peerlist Launchpad

* It will be visible to the entire Peerlist community

## Scheduling a Launch

In case you missed launching on Monday or aren't sure that you will make it in time for the next one, you can schedule a launch on any of the other days.

Simply,

<Steps>
  <Step>
    Open your project
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Click on the **Launch** button
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/launchpad/project-scheduling.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    From the modal, click on the **Schedule Launch • Week Num** button
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/launchpad/schedule-launch-modal.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step>
    And your launch will be scheduled!
    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/launchpad/scheduled-success.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
  Incase your project details aren't **100% complete**, your launch will fail. To avoid any failures, make sure that it is 100% complete before it launches on Monday.
</Info>

## After Launching

Once your project is live on Launchpad, you can:

* Share the project link with your network

* Engage with community feedback and comments

* Track views and interactions with your project

* Connect with others who show interest in your work

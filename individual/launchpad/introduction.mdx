## What is Peerlist Launchpad?

<a target="_blank" href={PEERLIST_EXTERNAL_LINKS.projects}>Peerlist Launchpad</a> is designed to showcase innovative and impactful products to a wide audience. It provides a unique opportunity for you to share your work with peers, receive valuable feedback, and potentially gain recognition for your efforts.

<Note>
  Launch window opens on **Monday** every week!
</Note>

## Why Participate?

Peerlist Launchpad not only provides you an opportunity for recognition but also fosters a sense of community and collaboration. By sharing your products/projects and receiving feedback from peers, you can improve your work and potentially collaborate with others on future projects.

The winners will be featured in Peerlist's monthly newsletter and social media channels, providing valuable exposure to the creators. Additionally, the winners will receive a special badge for their products, showcasing their achievements and potentially attracting new opportunities.

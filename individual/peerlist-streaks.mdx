The goal with streaks is to encourage meaningful collaboration and contribution. You add value, you get value!

<Info>
  Streaks are tracked based on Coordinated Universal Time (UTC), so resets may not align with your local timezone.
</Info>

## Streaks FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How contributions are calculated?">
    Streak is calculated based the contribution you do towards Peerlist community. Following actions can be considered as contribution:

    * When you share a new post.

    * When you launch a new project.

    * When you write and publish article.

    * When you engage with post and article shared by other.

    * When you share a thoughtful feedback on project launches.

    * When you upvote launched projects.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What are streak milestones?">
    There are milestones after every specific days (this may change time-to-time). Once reached, you will unlock rewards.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are there rewards on achieving milestones?">
    Absolutely, yes! At every milestone, you will unlock rewards. These rewards may change time-to-time.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is Super Upvote?">
    Peers with a 14+ day streak earn a Super Upvote! This means your upvote on any launched project will carry twice the weight of a regular upvote.

    Maintain your streak to enjoy 2x upvote weight on Product Launchpad.

    Peers with Super Upvote will also have a distinguished ⚡️ badge on their profile picture in supporter's widget.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What if my streak breaks? Can I restore it?">
    Yes you can restore your streak if you have achieved a 7-day milestone.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How to restore my streak?">
    If you have unlocked Restore Streak reward by achieving 7-day milestone you can restore your streak. But please note that,

    * You can recover up to 2 inactive days at most.

    * You can restore within 2 days from the day the streak broke. After that you won't be able restore and you have to start over.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
